Raw video test
=========================

Records RAW video.

This is a simplified reimplementation of raw video recording,
with very few features.

raw_vidX is eXperimental.  It's designed to be preserved as
a minimal example of raw video, for testing new ports, etc.

Symbol names are very likely to conflict with raw_vid module.
Do not have both modules on the same cam.
